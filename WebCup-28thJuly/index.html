World's Largest Aquarium ! A lifetime Experience
The Mauritius Aquarium welcomes you to a unique experience of relaxation and discovery!
Situated on the North West of Mauritius, in the charming small village of Pointe Aux Piments, the Mauritius Aquarium offers a unique experience.

A leisurely stroll through the five well laid out principal buildings surrounded by a tropical garden, allows its visitors to discover the rich aquatic life which inhabits the coastal and pelagic waters of Mauritius.

The aquarium is home to a large number of species.An added attraction offered by the Mauritius Aquarium is the possibility feeding sessions daily at 11:00 am and 15:00 pm. The Aquarium has adopted an ecologically-friendly system, known as a closed system, which involves the treatment and recycling of the seawater in the circuit linking the tanks.
